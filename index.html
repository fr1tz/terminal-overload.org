Terminal Overload is a home-made experimental free and open source multiplayer FPS game.
It is the continuation of ROTC: Ethernet and is currently in an early stage of development.

Latest public release: Version 0.3.1

Please read before downloading

In order to keep development going at a decent pace, TOL needs more devs or more patrons for its main dev. Please visit www.patreon.com/fr1tz for a more detailed explanation of the situation or consider becoming a dev.
